FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

Do I need to be technical?+

No. We handle the build, the hosting, the monitoring and the maintenance. You see the outcome and the dashboards — never the wiring.

How quickly will I see results?+

Most clients have a first automation live within 2–4 weeks of signing. The payback on the retainer typically lands inside the first quarter.

What if I want to cancel?+

30 days' notice, no exit fees. We hand over documentation and credentials at the end of the engagement.

What about data security & GDPR?+

We only use UK/EU data-resident providers where possible, sign DPAs, and document every data flow. You retain full ownership of your data and your automations.

Will AI replace my team?+

No — and that's not the goal. We automate the repetitive work so your people can focus on the conversations, decisions and relationships that grow the business.

How is this different from off-the-shelf SaaS?+

Off-the-shelf tools force your business into someone else's workflow. We build the workflow around your business — your tools, your data, your processes.
